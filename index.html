Pierogi

Description

Pierogi are a traditional dish eaten in Eastern Europe. They are filled dumplings, with unleavend dough wrapped around a variety of fillings. It is boiled in water, and can be pan-fried.

Ingredients

  • Dough
    • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup sour cream
    • 4 tablespoons butter, room temp
  • Filling
    • 1 cup mashed potatoes
    • 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese
