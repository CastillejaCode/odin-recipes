Cooked Rice

Description

Rice is a ubiquitous dish providing a simple yet hearty meal. This will be a simple serving of cooked rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 2 1/5 cups of water

Steps

  1. Rinse your rice until water is no longer cloudy.
  2. Strain all water out of rice.
  3. Pour rice and water into pot.
  4. Boil water, and afterwards set heat to simmer.
  5. Wait 13 minutes.
  6. Eat.
