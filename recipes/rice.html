Cooked Rice

Description

Pierogi are a traditional dish eaten in Eastern Europe. They are filled dumplings, with unleavend dough wrapped around a variety of fillings. It is boiled in water and can be pan-fried.

Ingredients

  • Dough
    • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup sour cream
    • 4 tablespoons butter, room temp
  • Filling
    • 1 cup mashed potatoes
    • 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Mix flour and salt. Mix in egg.
  2. Work in sour cream and soft butter until dough is in a sticky ball.
  3. Knead and fold dough.
  4. Wrap dough in plastic wrap and cool for 60 minutes.
  5. Combine mashed potatoes and cheese.
  6. Cut circles of dough. Place 1 1/2 teaspoons of filling.
  7. Fold dough over, pinching edges to seal and afterwards with a fork.
  8. Boil until floating.
  9. Eat.
